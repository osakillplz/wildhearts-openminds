Who I Am

Reena Giasi

Three years ago I stepped into education — working as a substitute teacher across schools in The Hague, from the youngest classes to the oldest. My curiosity about movement and psychology grew into something more: workshops that bring children, school teams, and families closer to themselves, and closer to each other.

Move in Presence is where that work lives for adults — playful, experience-led sessions that build rest, focus, and connection, on the mat and far beyond it.

The Flagship Workshop

Move in Presence

Not a class. Not therapy. A guided return to yourself.

Across three unhurried hours, guided movement opens space to notice the feelings, patterns and behaviours shaping this moment of your life — no experience necessary, just a willingness to show up.
