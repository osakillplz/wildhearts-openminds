Who I Am

Reena Giasi

Five years ago I stepped into education for kids, creating experiences as a teacher across schools in The Hague, the Netherlands. From the youngest classes to the oldest. My curiosity about movement and psychology grew into something more: workshops that bring children, parents, school teams, every human being that has a heartbeat closer to themselves, and closer to each other.

Move in Presence is where my work lives now for humans. A playful, experience-led session that builds rest, focus and connection. Through movement, stillness and everything in between.

The Flagship Workshop

Move in Presence

Not another class. Not another therapy. A guided journey into presence.
Just be. Breathe. Observe. By move and play.

1 / 7

Across three unhurried hours, guided movement opens space to notice the feelings, patterns and behaviours shaping this moment of your life. No experience necessary, just a willingness to show up however you feel. You are more than welcome with everything you are. Without words, with comfy clothes and barefoot.
